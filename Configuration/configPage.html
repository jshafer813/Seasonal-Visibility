🎄 Seasonal Visibility

Hide and show movies & TV shows based on seasonal tags. Content tagged with a season tag will be hidden from non-admin users outside of that season, and revealed during the season.

Season Rules

Each rule maps one or more tags (comma separated) to a date range (MM-DD format). Cross-year ranges are supported (e.g. Dec 1 – Jan 5).
