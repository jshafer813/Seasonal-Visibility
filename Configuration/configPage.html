🎄 Seasonal Visibility

Hide and show movies & TV shows based on seasonal tags. Content tagged with a season tag will be hidden from non-admin users outside of that season, and revealed during the season.

Season Rules

Each rule maps a tag to a date range (MM-DD format). Content tagged with that tag will only be visible during that window. Cross-year ranges are supported (e.g. Dec 1 – Jan 5).
